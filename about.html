What We Do

We hold a myriad of tournaments for card enthusiasts just like you! Do you love Magic the Gathering or Dungeons and Dragons? Then you'll love Battle & Dice! We host multiple formats for Magic and plenty of sessions of Dungeons and Dragons. And if you're not into tournaments, there are plenty of oppurtunities to play casually. We also provide product and card pricing guides to help you build the deck you want and get the most of your hard-earned money.
