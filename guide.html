Commander

Commander is a 4-player gamemode, in which every person brings a 100-card deck. Unlike other formats, players are limited to only 1 copy of a card in their deck, but are given 40 health instead of 20. Games are long given the nature of the format, but the outcome of a match is more unpredictable than the rest.

Booster Draft

The goal of the draft is to make a deck out of a random assortment of cards from the latest set. For an entry fee of $8, each player is given 3 booster packs and free lands. To draft, each player opens their pack, picks a card from their hand, and passes their cards to the other person. This is done, until no card remains. Players will have 30 minutes to prepare a 40-card deck.

Modern

Modern is the tradition 2-on-2 gamemode, where both players bring their own 60-card decks. Cards are limited to the eight latest expansions, allowing a great diversity of cards and variety. Ban lists are applied to this format.
